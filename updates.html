Performances/Concerts

NCT Concert

SM Entertainment announced that all members of NCT would be holding a full-group concert "NCT Nation: To The World". This includes NCT 127, NCT Dream, NCT U, NCT Dojaejung and WayV. The concert was held in both Korea and Japan from late August to mid September. Fans who weren't able to secure tickets to the concerts were also allowed to buy tickets for their upcoming movie that shows the concert and some behind the scenes footage.

Original Article: Soompi

Added dates to World Tour

In addition to their ongoing second world tour "The Dream Show 2", NCT Dream added a set of new dates and locations to their list of shows, including 2 more shows in Korea in June and a 4 more shows in Latin America in July. This brings their total number of shows for the tour to more than 23.

Original Article: NME

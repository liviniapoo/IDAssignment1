About NCT DREAM

NCT Dream debuted under SM Entertainment as the third sub-unit of boy-group NCT on August 25, 2016, as a 7-member group consisting of Mark, Renjun, Jeno, Haechan, Jaemin, Chenle and Jisung.

Meet the Dreamies

Mark

Stage Name: Mark (마크)

Name: Mark Lee Min Hyung (이민형)

Birthday: August 2, 1999

Positions: Leader, Main Rapper, Main Dancer, FOTG

Instagram: @onyourm__ark

Fun Facts: Born and raised in Canada, Mark joined as an SM Rookie in 2013 and made multiple debuts in groups NCT U, NCT 127, NCT Dream and SuperM. In 2022, Mark made a solo debut with the single "Child".

Renjun

Stage Name: Renjun (런쥔)

Name: Huang Renjun (黄仁俊)

Birthday: March 23, 2000

Positions: Main Vocalist, Lead Dancer

Instagram: @yellow_3to3

Fun Facts: Renjun was born in Jilin, China and he's very fluent in Mandarin and Korean. He understands English pretty well. Renjun is known to be very interested in philosophical topics.

Jeno

Stage Name: Jeno (제노)

Name: Lee Je No (이제노)

Birthday: April 23, 2000

Positions: Lead Dancer, Lead Rapper, Visual

Instagram: @leejen_o_423

Fun Facts: Born in Incheon, Korea, Jeno has been in the entertainment industry every since he was little. He was a child actor involved in filming commercials for different brands. Funnily enough, although him and his family own 3 cats, Jeno himself is allergic to cats.

Haechan

Stage Name: Haechan (해찬)

Name: Lee Dong Hyuck (이동혁)

Birthday: June 6, 2000

Positions: Main Vocalist

Instagram: @haechanahceah

Fun Facts: Haechan was born in Seoul, Korea and grew up in Jeju for a couple years. Haechan is also a member of NCT 127 alongside Mark where he is the youngest. Haechan is most known for his outgoing personality and honey-like vocals.

Jaemin

Stage Name: Jaemin (재민)

Name: Na Jae Min (나재민)

Birthday: August 13, 2000

Positions: Lead Dancer, Sub Rapper, Sub Vocalist, Visual

Instagram: @na.jaemin0813

Fun Facts: Jaemin was born and raised in Seoul as a single child. He was put on a one year hiatus in 2017 due to a herniated disc hence he missed out on the group's first and second comeback. He returned during the group's promotions for "GO" under the NCT 2018 project.

Chenle

Stage Name: Chenle (천러 )

Name: Zhong Chenle (钟辰乐 / 鍾辰樂)

Birthday: November 22, 2001

Positions: Main Vocalist

Instagram: @kh1000le

Fun Facts: Chenle is the grandson of one of Shanghai's top entreprenuers, hence making him one of the richest members of NCT. He is known to be an incredibly talented singer, having hosted his own concert as a kid and he debuted in NCT Dream after just 2 months of training.

Jisung

Stage Name: Jisung (지성)

Name: Park Ji Sung (박지성)

Birthday: February 5, 2002

Positions: Main Dancer, Sub Vocalist, Sub Rapper, Maknae

Instagram: @the__and.y

Fun Facts: Jisung was raised in Busan but started out in the entertainment industry incredibly young. He was a part of a children's skit for a few episodes, played a role in an old Korean movie and more. He also participated in dance-related reality shows to show off his passion.
